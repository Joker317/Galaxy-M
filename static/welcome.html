Galaxy-M

Here we present our end-to-end mass spectrometry metabolomics pipeline in Galaxy, the widely used workflow platform. Galaxy is intuitive to use and highly flexible allowing non-programmers to create analysis pipelines from a broad and expanding suite of tools. Further, the workflow is recorded and can be shared and reproduced easily. Our pipeline has been developed for both direct infusion mass spectrometry (DIMS) and liquid chromatography mass spectrometry (LC-MS) metabolomics. The range of tools presented spans from the processing of raw data, e.g. peak picking and alignment, and proceeds through data cleansing, e.g. missing value imputation, to preparation for statistical analysis, e.g. normalisation and scaling, before providing the ubiquitous principal components analysis (PCA) with associated statistical evaluation. This provides a robust backbone for a mass spectrometry based metabolomics study.
